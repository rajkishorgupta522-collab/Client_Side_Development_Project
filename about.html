About HealthSync

HealthSync is a comprehensive symptom analysis platform designed to help individuals better understand their health concerns.

Our mission is to empower people with knowledge about their symptoms and possible conditions, while always emphasizing the importance of professional medical consultation.

Developed as a university project for BSc Computing Systems, HealthSync demonstrates the practical application of web technologies in the healthcare domain.

Our Features

  • Symptom tracking and analysis
  • Possible condition matching
  • Health recommendations
  • User-friendly interface
  • Secure name-based authentication
